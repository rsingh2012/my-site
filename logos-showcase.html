Usage notes

Option 1 — When to use

Best for: website nav, email signatures, presentation headers, anywhere the mark + name together read clearly. The icon is distinctive enough to stand alone at larger sizes once the brand is established.

Option 2 — When to use

Best for: proposal covers, conference materials, LinkedIn banner, professional documents. The italic signature quality gives it authority without an icon. Works well large — less ideal at small sizes.

Colors used

Amber/brass #c49a3c — mark & accent.
Warm ivory #ede8df — primary text on dark.
Dark navy #0b1522 — primary text on light.
Slate #7a95b4 — credentials, secondary.
